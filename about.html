The Origin

We made the box we always wanted but could never find.

The Rima Box started with a simple frustration: why does every stash solution look like it belongs under a mattress? Ziplock bags, beat-up tins, flimsy acrylic cases — none of it matched the quality of what was going inside.

We designed the Rima Box as the antithesis of all that. Something that sits on your shelf. Something you're proud to pull out. A box that says you take your ritual seriously.

Every detail was chosen with that in mind — the indestructible plexiglass shell, the velvet lining, the ceramic grinder, the rare earth magnets that snap shut with that satisfying click.
